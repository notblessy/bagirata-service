Bagirata Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 July 2024

At Bagirata, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Camera Access:

Bagirata requires access to your device's camera to scan receipts and bills. The images captured are used solely for the purpose of bill splitting and are not shared with third parties.

Internet Access:

Bagirata requires an internet connection to allow you to share split bills with your friends via link. We do not collect or store any personal data related to this sharing process.

Usage Data:

We may collect anonymous usage data to help improve the app's performance and user experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

Camera Data:

Images captured using the app's camera feature are used exclusively to process and split bills. These images are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Internet Access:

Internet access is used to facilitate sharing of split bills. We do not collect or store any personal data during this process.

Usage Data:

Anonymous usage data helps us understand how users interact with Bagirata, enabling us to make improvements and enhance the app's functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
